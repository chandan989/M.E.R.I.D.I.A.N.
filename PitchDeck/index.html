How It Works

A Four-Step Revolution

01

Connect with Web5

Create your decentralized identity and personal data vault (DWN) in seconds. True self-sovereignty starts here.

02

Upload Your Data

Securely upload datasets to your personal DWN. Your data is encrypted and never leaves your control.

03

AI Quality Check

Grant temporary, revocable access to our AI for quality scoring and fair pricing suggestions, without compromising privacy.

04

Mint & List

Transform your data's access rights into a tradable NFT on the blockchain, set your price, and list it on the marketplace.
